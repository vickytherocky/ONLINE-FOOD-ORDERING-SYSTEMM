About Us

"Your gateway to delightful food experiences."

Food-Ordering.com is a standalone web application for hospitality businesses worldwide, which allows direct, efficient, fully owned, and controlled sales and engagement with customers across different functions (online ordering, in-store ordering, telephone ordering with CallerID, table booking with preordering) and languages (supports 144 languages).

Hungry for sales? Thirsty for productivity? Take your food and drink orders online with Jotform’s Food and Beverage Order Form Templates. You can view or share order submissions on any device, making it easy to keep staff members in the loop. Spice up your form design with our Form Builder — add mouth-watering photos and descriptions of your products to excite customers. Why not speed up the process even further and accept secure payments online via Square, Stripe, or PayPal? Get organized, streamline your workflow, and make sure you never miss another order thanks to Jotform’s Food and Beverage Order Form Templates.
